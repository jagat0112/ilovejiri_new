WHERE IS JIRI, ANYWAY?

Not familiar with Nepalese geography? Jiri is on the central Nepal, only 180 km of ride away from Kathmandu. Some people think it's the best thing about the Bagmati Pradesh, and of course they're right.

Made up of seven diverse regions, Jiri has beautiful hills, mountains, valleys, high desert, small towns, hemlets ,and almost everything in between.
